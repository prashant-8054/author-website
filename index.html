About

The writer
behind the words

I'm an SFF writer based in Muzaffarpur, Bihar, India. I've written middle-grade novels previously and have since moved into darker, more complex territory — adult fantasy with teeth.

Right now I'm querying Grotesqueen (GQ), a dark adult fantasy, while simultaneously building a new adult fantasy world from the ground up.

When I'm not writing, I'm teaching myself to code. I've finished JavaScript and I'm currently working through ReactJS — because apparently one obsessive craft isn't enough.

Current Project

Grotesqueen

Dark Adult Fantasy  ·  Currently Querying

When a hunted blood mage teams up with a corpse-bound god to kill the lord who murdered her siblings, she accidentally creates a new magic that scatters the god across the dead — triggering an undead uprising she must stop before innocent children burn for her revenge.

In pursuit of the unknown, we're doomed to stay one step behind.
— Prashant Singh
